Addmission process


2023 is starting from 15th July 2022. Generally 175-200 seats are reserved for students of first year (Science, Commerce, Arts, BBA, Technology, Engineering and similar) streams. 11th, 12th and Post Graduation students should not apply. Students can apply for remaining sections and other years. But the decision to call them for interview will be by the committee as per available seats. Online applications will be pre-scrutinized and a decision will be made to call for an interview. While coming for the interview, you should bring the complete application form of the committee and all the original documents along with it on the scheduled day of the interview. At least one parent must be present. Students are expected to have complete information about the committee's student related activities . Confirm admission by filling. Or it will be considered cancelled. The full authority and decisions of the Committee regarding admission shall be binding on all applicants.
Information Sheet ( 2022 )
The aim of the Vidyarthi Sahayak Committee is to provide accommodation and food at a low cost to the poor, intelligent and promising students coming to Pune from rural areas for higher education and to develop their personality and to give them the touch of entrepreneurship. So that the students can get education easily, they can get proper support and guidance and stand on their own feet with self-reliance. This work was started in 1955 under the guidance of Achyutarao Apte. There are five hostels of the institute, two for students and three for female students, and 800 students are benefiting from these facilities. A branch of the committee has also been started in Ahmednagar. The organization is running on financial support from donors and small fees from students. cleanliness, Equality and labor dignity are the trinity of the organization. The organization is making continuous efforts to make responsible citizens for the country. Seats are available to male and female students for fresh admission every year. Admission to each session is by viewing the hostel. After 10th, Diploma, I. T. Admission is given to courses after I., XII. The academic qualification for admission should be at least 60%.
